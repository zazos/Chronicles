### Strategy

1. **Support:** Use healing spells and buffs like _Bless_, _Cure Wounds_, and _Beacon of Hope_ to keep allies in fighting shape.
2. **Combat:** Use _Spiritual Weapon_ for consistent damage and _Sacred Flame_ for ranged attacks. Use _Radiance of the Dawn_ for AoE damage and to dispel darkness.
3. **Control:** Use _Hold Person_ to incapacitate dangerous foes and _Warding Flare_ to protect yourself and allies.
4. **Utility:** Use _Lesser Restoration_ to remove conditions and _Dispel Magic_ to counter enemy spells.
